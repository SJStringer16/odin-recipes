Spaghetti

Description

This homemade recipe creates a classic dish of spaghetti with meat-sauce that will fill any hungry stomach.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1 lb. Ground Beef or Venison
  • 1 Jar of Spaghetti Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a medium pot. Add salt to taste.
  2. While you wait on the water to boil, brown the ground meat in a skillet.
  3. Once the water has come to a boil, add the noodles to the water.
  4. When the meat is brown, drain the grease and add the sauce to the same skillet.
  5. After the noodles have finished cooking, drain the water.
  6. Put some noodles in a small bowl to serve.
  7. Add the meat-sauce on top of the noodles.
  8. Top it off with some permesan cheese.
  9. Enjoy!
