Hamburgers

Description

Classic homemade hamburgers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • Sliced Cheese of Preference
  • 3-4 Buns
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Head of Lettuce
  • 1 Onion
  • Steak Seasoning

Steps

  1. Start up the grill, and allow it to heat up while completing steps 2 and 3.
  2. Form 1 lb. of ground beef into 3-4 patties.
  3. Season both sides of the patties with steak seasoning.
  4. Place the patties on the grill and cook each side for 4 minutes on medium heat.
  5. While the burgers are cooking, slice your tomato and onion.
  6. When the burgers look like they are about done, add a slice of cheese per patty.
  7. Remove from heat once cheese has melted.
  8. Place each patty on a bun.
  9. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and your choice of condiments onto the burger.
  10. Enjoy!
